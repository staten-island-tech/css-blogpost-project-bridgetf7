Teas Around the World

Chrysanthemum Tea

Chrysanthemum tea is made by steeping dried chrysanthemum flowers in boiling the Song Dynasty and is known for several medicinal benefits. Namely, it is known to improve high blood sugar and treat osteoporosis.

Green Tea

Green tea is considered one of the most health beneficial teas out there. Ancient Chinese and Indian medicine frequently used this teat to aid digestion, improve heart health, and regulate body temperature. In modern times, scientists believe that it can have a positive effect on weight loss.

Chai

"Chai" is a Hindi word, meaning "tea". The tea itself is a mix of spices, which are steeped into a homologus beverage. Traditionally, chai si a black tea steeped with cinnamon, cardamon, and ginger.It is then mixed with milk and sweetened with sugar.
