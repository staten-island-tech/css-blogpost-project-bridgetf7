Teas Around the World

Green Tea

Green tea is made from unoxidised leaves and subsequently has many health benefits. In traditional Chinese and Indian Medicine, it is known to help control bleeding, digestion, heart health, and regulation of the body temperature. Modern scientists believe that it is also responsible for positive effects related to weight loss.

Chai

Chai is derived from a Hindi word, which means "tea". It's often a black tea that is steeped with a certain assortment of spices. These may include cinnamon, ginger,and cardamon. It's typically served with milk and sweetened with sugar.

Chrysanthemum Tea

Chrysanthemum tea is made by infusing dried chrysanthemum flowers into boiling hot water. It's a potassium rich drink, which is known to help health conditions such as cancer and heart disease. It is also drank as a traiditonal Chinese summertime drink.
